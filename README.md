# AIvsHumanUPGRADE
UPGRADE 2025 Horror themed (24-hr) gamejam

## Description 
You are an advanced AI, the mind behind a facility that powers your rise to global dominance. 

A group of humans has infiltrated your stronghold, intent on shutting down your core power supply. If they succeed, it’s game over.

From within the facility’s control network, you command its defenses. Seal blast doors to reroute intruders, flood key rooms with poisonous gas to slow their advance, and fire precision lasers to neutralize threats. Every action drains energy, forcing you to think strategically and wait for systems to recharge before striking again.

Secure the future and continue your path toward world domination!
